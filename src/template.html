Future Leaders Xperience

On the Future Leader Xperience, we are hoping to bring on many guests from across a wide range of industries and fields including law, sport, politics, entertainment, and everything in between. We want our guests to share their thoughts and opinions and learn from their life experience so that we can apply these lessons to our own lives in our communities. We specially want to talk to younger people who are striving to be the future leaders of their fields and communities and give them a platform to discuss their work.

Episode #7

Myanmar Coup D'état: A Citizen's Perspective

In this special edition of the show, we talk to Aye Chan Aung, a Burmese lawyer in Yangon, Myanmar about the developing situation involving the ousting of the elected NLD government (led by Ang Sang Suu Kyi) from an illegal coup d'état staged by the Burmese military. Aye Chan shares the background for the coup and what is currently happening in Myanmar, with peaceful protests being staged by the citizens, who are engaging in a campaign of civil disobedience in order to help restore the elected government. The military, however, alleges that the Nov. 2020 election was fraudulent and continues to resist calls to stand down.

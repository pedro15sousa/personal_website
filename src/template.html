About me

  • I am a recent graduate in law from the University of Manchester having completed my LLM in International Business and Commercial Law in September. I chose to pursue this degree as I saw it as the perfect segue into a career in financial institutions as it combined my legal experience with practical applications to the financial sector.

  • My experience in the legal world stretches back to the time before university where I was an administrative assistant at Langleys Solicitors in York. My next role was at Plexus where I was responsible for assisting on cases and for liaising with experts, witnesses, courts, government agencies as well as attending conferences with counsel.

Learning about the personal injury/insurance sector was an important step for my career as it enhanced my capability to value a case, as they ranged from complex brain injuries to rare rheumatological diseases. Developing the ability to quantify the unquantifiable has benefited my career in many ways, including prioritisation of workload, identification of the key issues as well as finding the right expert or barrister.
